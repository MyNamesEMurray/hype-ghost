Connect the brain.

Hype Ghost generates messages with the Claude API. Get a key at console.anthropic.com — typical cost is about $0.20/hour of streaming.

Let the ghost see your stream.

In OBS: Tools → WebSocket Server Settings → enable the server, then copy the password here. This is how Hype Ghost screenshots your live scene to react to it.

OBS not running right now? You can skip this — it reconnects automatically.

How chatty should the ghost be?
ChattyAverages about a message a minute — with natural bursts and lulls either side.
BalancedAverages about every 90 seconds. Good default.
RelaxedEvery 2–3 minutes — present, but leaves you room.

When real viewers are present the ghost automatically slows to ~4x this gap and shifts to prompts that help you engage the room.
